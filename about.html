About finsplit

Simplifying Group Expenses, One Split at a Time

Managing shared expenses with friends, family, or colleagues can quickly become complicated. Finsplit was created to take the stress and confusion out of splitting bills, offering an intuitive, transparent, and fair way to handle shared costs. Whether it's a group dinner, vacation, or rent with roommates, Finsplit ensures everyone pays their fair share—no hassle, no headaches.

Why Choose finsplit?

  • Fair and Flexible: No matter how unique your situation is, Finsplit allows you to customize how expenses are split.
  • Effortless: Add expenses, see who owes what, and settle up in just a few clicks.
  • Transparent: Everyone in the group knows what they owe and can track payments easily.
  • Time-Saving: Forget the complicated calculations. Finsplit does the math for you, making the process quick and simple.

Our Vision

Our goal is to continue simplifying group expenses for people everywhere. We aim to provide an easy-to-use platform that eliminates awkward financial conversations and builds transparency into every interaction. Finsplit’s mission is to make the process of splitting bills as stress-free as possible, whether you're at a restaurant, on vacation, or sharing household expenses.

Our Story

The idea for finsplit came from the common struggle we all face when splitting bills. After countless dinners, trips, and shared expenses where things got confusing, our team decided there had to be a better way. We envisioned a solution that not only split bills fairly but also made the process smooth and automatic. What began as a simple idea is now a powerful tool used by thousands of people to make group expenses a breeze.
