Our Services

Group Bill Splitting

Create groups for shared expenses like dining, vacations, or household bills. Finsplit lets you split bills fairly among group members, with customizable options for equal or proportional splits.

Benefit: Simplifies bill splitting for any group, ensuring transparency and fairness.

Expense Tracking

Track all group expenses in one place with a real-time expense log. Every member can view shared costs and monitor who owes what.

Benefit: Keeps everyone on the same page and avoids misunderstandings about money owed.

Automated Settlements

Finsplit automatically calculates and suggests how to settle up among group members to minimize the number of transactions required.

Benefit: Saves time and reduces the hassle of multiple payments or awkward money transfers.

Recurring Payments

Automate recurring expenses like rent, utilities, or subscriptions with monthly tracking and automatic reminders.

Benefit: Ensures regular expenses are never forgotten, making it easier to share the burden of ongoing costs.

Multi-Currency Support

Handle bills in multiple currencies, especially useful for international trips. Finsplit automatically converts and adjusts based on current exchange rates.

Benefit: Makes it easy to split expenses across borders without manual currency conversion.

Debt Simplification

Instead of multiple payments between all group members, Finsplit consolidates payments, suggesting the easiest way for everyone to settle their debts with minimal transfers.

Benefit: Reduces the number of transactions needed to settle group bills.

Mobile App Integration

Sync your Finsplit account with our mobile app, allowing you to track and manage group expenses on the go, anytime, anywhere.

Benefit: Full functionality available via mobile, making it easy to split bills no matter where you are.

Expense Categorization

Categorize expenses (e.g., food, travel, rent) to get a better understanding of where the group's money is going.

Benefit: Helps users see spending patterns and manage budgets more effectively.

Expense Reports and Summaries

Generate detailed reports of group expenses, including individual contributions, overall group costs, and payment summaries.

Benefit: Provides a clear financial overview, perfect for budgeting or reviewing past expenses.

Data Privacy & Security

Finsplit offers end-to-end encryption and robust data protection to ensure all financial information is safe.

Benefit: Users can trust that their sensitive financial data is secure.
